About

I am Alex Szczesiul an aspirirng climber currently on the brink of V6 bouldering and 11a lead climbing. I love the outdoors and climbing for me is a new way to connect with the outside world, it gives me and hopefully will give you a better understanding and respect for the outside world. I am an enviorenmental activist, and love hiking and bouldering. My favorite type of hold is pinchers and crimps, I enjoy using crimps for more technical styles of climbing, and love using pinchers for the bigger and stronger moves, as well as jugs. Slopers are my worst enemy but I understand how to use them and slip off. My favorite shoes are the Scarpa Instinct VS. Admist the current pandemic I have been using liquid chalk, however I prefer normal chalk. Slab is also my favorite style of climbing, wither its the technical moves and the small holds, or the adrenaline from being hundreds of feet up on a quarter pad crimp, slab is the most satisfying climb to me, and the coolest to watch. From my journey from beginner to a mid level climber I am here to share my expirence and knowledge with you.
